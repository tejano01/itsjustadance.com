Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 5, 2026  ·  Last updated: May 8, 2026

The short version: It's Just A Dance is built around privacy by design. We collect only what's necessary to connect you with a dance partner at a venue. Your session data is deleted when you leave. We do not sell or share your personal information. We don't show your photo, and we don't let anyone message you.

1. Who we are

It's Just A Dance ("we," "us," or "our") is an iOS application developed and operated by Michael Rivera, based in Texas, United States. You can reach us at support@itsjustadance.com.

2. What information we collect

  • Account information: Email address (authentication only), username (visible only to previous dance partners), age verification (18+ confirmation), and profile preferences — dance styles, skill level, role preference, gender identity, and gender preference.
  • Session data: Temporary record of your assigned color, venue location, and active match requests. Discarded when you check out.
  • Location data: GPS used only to detect nearby registered venues. We do not store your location history.
  • Ratings: Post-dance ratings stored permanently and tied to your account. Rating tags are aggregated anonymously.
  • Dance history: Permanent log of completed dances (partner ID, venue, date).
  • Push notification tokens: Used to send mutual match notifications to your device.
  • Crash reports: Anonymous crash data via Firebase Crashlytics to improve app stability.

3. What we never collect

  • Photos or images of any kind — ever
  • Your real name
  • Messages between users (there is no messaging feature)
  • Precise location history or movement tracking
  • Social media profiles or third-party account data
  • Payment information (handled entirely by Apple)
  • Sensitive personal information such as financial data, health information, Social Security numbers, or government-issued ID numbers

4. How we use your information

  • To operate the app: Match you with compatible dancers, display the browse screen, send match notifications, and record ratings.
  • To improve the app: Aggregate, anonymous analytics on dance styles, venue activity, and app performance. No individual-level behavioral tracking.
  • To communicate with you: Transactional emails only (password reset, account confirmation). No marketing emails without explicit consent.
  • To ensure safety: Permanent ratings tied to accounts to deter and identify bad actors.

5. We do not sell or share your personal information

We do not sell, rent, trade, or share your personal information with third parties for their own marketing or commercial purposes. This applies to all users, including residents of California and other states with specific data sale restrictions.

We share data only with service providers who help us operate the app (listed in Section 9), and only to the extent necessary to provide those services.

6. Session ephemerality

When you check out — manually, by leaving the venue geofence, or through automatic expiration — your session data is permanently discarded. This includes your assigned color, active match requests, and browse history. This is by design and cannot be reversed.

Data retained across sessions: account, email, username, shape, dance styles, skill level, preferences, cumulative ratings, and dance history.

7. Gender preference filtering

Your gender preference is applied as a mutual compatibility filter entirely on our servers. It is never exposed to other users, never displayed in the app UI, and never transmitted beyond what is required to show compatible dancers.

8. Ratings and account permanence

Ratings are tied permanently to your account UUID — not your session identity. This is an intentional safety feature that prevents bad actors from evading accountability. Low-rated dancers are not explicitly penalized — they simply do not receive a rating badge.

9. Data sharing and service providers

  • Supabase: Backend database (PostgreSQL). Data stored in US West (Oregon).
  • Apple: Push notifications (APNs) and in-app purchases (StoreKit). Governed by Apple's privacy policy.
  • Google Firebase: Crash reporting (Crashlytics). Anonymous data only.
  • Google AdMob: Venue-sponsored advertisements for non-subscriber users. AdMob may use device identifiers per Google's privacy policy. Subscribers see no ads.
  • Venue operators: Registered venue owners and managers may access aggregated, anonymous reports about activity at their venue through the venue dashboard. These reports show aggregate metrics only — for example, total check-ins, dance style popularity, peak hours, and average session length. No individual dancer is identified in venue reports. Venue operators cannot see your username, shape, rating, gender, or any personal information. They see only anonymous aggregate statistics about collective activity at their location.

10. Venue analytics and reporting

It's Just A Dance provides registered venue operators with access to aggregated, anonymous analytics about activity at their venue. This is a core feature of the service — venues use this data to understand their dance community and market their events.

What venue operators can see:

  • Total number of dancers checked in per night
  • Dance style popularity breakdown (e.g. 61% country, 39% Latin)
  • Peak activity hours
  • Lead/follow ratio of checked-in dancers
  • Skill level distribution
  • Number of dances completed per night
  • Return visit rate — percentage of dancers who have visited before
  • Average session length

What venue operators cannot see:

  • Any individual dancer's identity, username, or shape
  • Individual ratings or rating history
  • Gender identity or gender preference
  • Who matched with whom
  • Any personally identifiable information about any dancer

All venue reports are aggregate and anonymous. A minimum threshold of 5 check-ins is enforced before any report is generated — preventing identification of individuals on low-traffic nights.

Venue operators are contractually required to keep dashboard data confidential and may not use aggregate data in ways that could identify individual dancers.

11. In-app purchases

Subscriptions are processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. We never see, store, or handle your payment information.

12. Data retention and deletion

  • Session data: Discarded on checkout or expiration (maximum 12 hours).
  • Account data: Retained for the life of your account.
  • Ratings and dance history: Retained permanently.
  • Account deletion: Email support@itsjustadance.com. We will delete your account, profile, and associated data within 30 days. Ratings received from others are anonymized but not deleted as they belong to the rater's record.

13. Children's privacy

It's Just A Dance is not intended for anyone under 18. We require users to confirm they are 18 or older during account creation. By creating an account, users affirmatively represent that they meet this age requirement. We rely on this self-attestation and do not independently verify age. If we become aware that a user is under 18, we will terminate their account immediately. If you believe a minor has created an account, contact support@itsjustadance.com.

14. Security

We use industry-standard security practices including row-level security policies on all database tables, encrypted connections (TLS/HTTPS), server-side filtering to prevent unauthorized data access, and security-definer database functions to prevent cross-user data exposure. We collect the minimum data necessary to operate the service.

In the event of a data breach affecting your personal information, we will notify affected users as required by applicable law.

Your rights. Under UK GDPR and EU GDPR, you have the right to:
